WHAT WE DO

This foundation is a group of professionals, comprising of a team of scientists , doctors , management graduates , software professionals , people who work for social cause ,psychologist ,gerontologist to offer the best and contiuing care for elderly. The team of like minded people who came together to set up "For the Oldies, a senior citizen home" in order to realize the concept of "community living and assisted care".

** Furnished self contained rooms, 24 hour nursing and personal care.
** Help with daily activities available.
** 24 hour staffing and on call medical services.
** Routine medical check up and administering medication, expert visits from required fields.
